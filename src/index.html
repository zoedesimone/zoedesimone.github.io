I’m Zoe, a creative technologist & researcher, currently studying at MIT.
I am pusuing a Master in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering & Building Technology @ MIT, in Cambridge Massachussets, where I am a National Science Foundation (NSF) Graduate Research Fellow. I am working under Prof. Christoph Reinhart on Urban Building Energy Modeling to help cities meet emission reduction targets and increase clean energy adoption rates. I recently completed my bachelor's in Architecture at Cornell University, where I combined my passions for tool development and design and wrote my undergraduate thesis on integrating machine learning with particle simulation for building design. Most recently, I worked in the Applied Research and Development team at Foster + Partners in London, where I helped develop tools to improve design collaboration using AR/VR for the AEC industry. On the side, I’ve led research projects with the Environmental Systems Lab and Robotics Construction Lab at Cornell, where I was responsible for the full lifecycle of product development, including ideation, design, research, development, implementation and validation.
